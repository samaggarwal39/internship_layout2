CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE


Welcome to St Francis of Assisi Catholic Technology College. Our faith is at the heart of everything we do and our motto is “Nil satis optimum”, only the best is enough. As a faith community, we have high expectations of ourselves.

Adults and students alike take a great pride in their work and in our great school. We have as our patron saint, St Francis of Assisi, whose brown religious habit is reflected in the colour of our uniform. Our students wear the Cross on their ties and blazers showing that they are ambassadors for the Catholic Church as well as for our school.

We work together as a team and encourage our young people to be proactive and have a voice in the development of our community. We respect and value every individual and celebrate their uniqueness and their personal contribution to our school. Our website offers you a glimpse of life at St Francis as well as a wealth of information and support for our students and their parents. Please let us know if there is anything you would like us to add to strengthen our partnership with you and regularly check our website for important notices and events.


Ms. Hanrahan

Headteacher
